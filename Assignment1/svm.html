Describe what your visualized SVM weights look like, and offer a brief explanation for why they look they way that they do.

$\color{blue}{\textit Your Answer:}$ The weights describe the best 'template' for the classes - For eg., the Frog class has a lot of green in the middle in comparsion to others. The horse class seems to have two heads since it the datapoints can have horses facing both directrions. The ship class is mainly blue since it is always surrounded by water and the sky. All classes look this way since the Linear SVM tries to maximize the margin between the right class and other classes.
